Weibo Extention

Social update at your desktop browser

Background

Extensions are small code packages that you can add to your browser to extend its functionality. It is built using open standards (HTML5, CSS, JavaScript) and supported APIs. At the time I joined Opera in July 2011, there were 718 extensions and 80 Speeddial extensions.

Role

Back in the year 2011, I designed and developed 5+ speed-dial extensions for Opera desktop browser. Among them Sina Weibo extension reached 200,000 downloads.

Product Features

Sina Weibo extension is developed to pull messages from my account and saved me the effort of checking my timeline now and then. It updated its content in a pre-defined time cycle and showed a notification bar on your speeddial window if new message was coming in.

Check out the Sina Weibo Extension

Design Process

Initially I joined Opera Software as Desktop QA engineer, after one-month product orientation, I took the initiative and started to build extensions for Opera Desktop.

  • Firstly I defined what I wanted to build. As Weibo (Chinese Twitter) started to gain popularity, I noticed the demand for a convenient way to check its update.
  • To enhance the browsing experience without user refreshing the website for updates, I chose to pushes notifications to users. I decided to work on speeddial extensions so users could access the information easier without any click.
  • Then I defined the features and tested internally in our office. After that, I did a few iterations and then submitted it through my personal developer account on Opera extension store.
  • After publishing I got a few feedback from users. I responded and updated the extension a few times to address the comments.

Outcome


The extension turned out to be a success: it got a few 5-star reviews and 200,000+ downloads. Then I was approached by my colleague in Norway to promote the extension. Moreover, on Chinese user community forum, people liked it and shared it with friends.

From this project, I found myself enjoying the role of designing a product, it enabled me to make an impact at a high level. Knowing that many users are relying on my product also give me a strong sense of accomplishment.
